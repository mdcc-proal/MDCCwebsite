    Our Privacy Policy

    MDCC International believes in being open and up front about how we use and look after your information. We want to assure you that your personal information is safe with us and we will never sell your details. Our privacy policy covers how we treat your data and put you in control of what happens to it.

    We are committed to protecting your personal information provided via our online platforms or given to us in other ways. You may provide personal information when you request or acquire a product or service from MDCC International, provide a product or service to MDCC International, complete a survey or questionnaire, participate in forums or when you communicate with us by e-mail, telephone or in writing e.g. if you contact customer service or provide feedback.

    This privacy policy explains the following:

    • What information MDCC International may collect about you
    • How MDCC International will use information we collect about you
    • When MDCC International may use your details to contact you
    • Whether MDCC International will disclose your details to anyone else
    • How long MDCC International will keep personal information
    • The use of cookies on MDCC International’s website and how you can reject these cookies

    MDCC International may, from time to time, review and update this privacy policy statement to take into account new laws and technology as well as changes to MDCC International’s operations. Please visit this page periodically to check for updates.

    What information MDCC International may collect about you

    MDCC International collects personal information from a variety of sources, including from members of the public, advertisers, prospective employees, mailing lists, recruitment agencies, contractors and business partners.

    In general, the personal information MDCC International collects includes (but is not limited to) name, address, contact details, gender, occupation, in some cases membership of professional associations and, where relevant, certain financial information (each such item being "Personal Information").


    How MDCC International will use the information we collect about you

    MDCC International may use Personal Information for the primary purpose for which it is collected and for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection.

    MDCC International generally uses personal information to:

    • Provide you with products or services you have requested

    • To personalise and customise your experiences on our website

    MDCC International may use Personal Information to communicate with you and to provide you with ongoing information about opportunities in which MDCC International believes you may be interested.

    In most cases where MDCC International asks you to provide Personal Information, if you do not provide that information, MDCC International will not be able to provide you with the requested product or service.

    When MDCC International may use your details to contact you

    MDCC International may contact you:

    • In relation to the functioning of any service you have signed up for in order to ensure that MDCC International can deliver the services to you

    • Where you have opted to receive further correspondence

    • For marketing purposes if you have requested to opt in to future communications from MDCC International.

    Whether MDCC International will disclose your information to anyone else

    MDCC International may disclose personal information to other companies in MDCC International that may only be used as set out above.

    How long will MDCC International keep personal information

    We will hold your personal information on our systems for as long as is necessary for the relevant service, or as long as is set out in any relevant contract you hold with MDCC International.

    The use of cookies on MDCC International website and how you can reject these cookies

    We may also use a feature of your browser called a cookie to assign your computer a 'User ID'. Cookies contain information by which MDCC International can identify your computer to our servers. You may configure your browser so that you are notified before a cookie is downloaded or so that your browser does not accept cookies. MDCC International may use information contained in cookies to make assumptions about the user of the computer. In some cases, a web page might contain more than one cookie. The information gathered from these different cookies can be used to build statistical and demographical information about the user of that computer.

    To disable your cookies, please refer to the help menu within your Internet browser.
