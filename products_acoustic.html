Acoustic Absorbing Panels

Our collection of acoustic absorbing panels is developed to absorb and reduce all kinds of disturbing noises in a room, leading to an increased acoustic comfort, even in rooms with large audiences and a lot of ambient noise. The main objective is to enhance and improve sound quality.It is worth noting that “sound absorption” is different than “soundproofing”, which is typically used to keep sound from escaping a room.

Our acoustic absorbing panels come in the form of closet walls, or mounted as wall cladding, partitions, sliding doors and interior doors, offering acoustic solutions for offices, call centers, meeting rooms, schools, reception halls, sports halls, restaurants, pubs, recording studios, living rooms, home theatres, museums, malls and other spaces.

These panels are scratchproof and are equipped with a core made of moisture repellent black MDF, which gives a refined and timeless design to our products and is far better option than the standard brown MDF. The top layer can be wiped with a wet towel and household detergents.

Moreover, these panels can be custom made according to your plans. And the top layer can be chosen from Abet Laminati Print HPL, veneer, lacquer, digital print or ntgrate® premium woven vinyl.
